it was a dime   
out of a dime  
a good time  
with easy rhyme  
pulled from the dark  
back to the spark   
might've missed the mark    
but we're still in the park   
im glad for the selection  
it was my type of affection    
maybe the risk of rejection  
is the cost for connection
